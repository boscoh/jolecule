jolecule [beta]

This is a new type of protein viewer that is completely web-based. No more painful plugins. It works entirely in the browser (CHROME, SAFARI and FIREFOX).

Jolecule is designed to make it easy to find and look at and identify residues and atoms.

It is easy to save what you are looking at, and with the built-in interpolation animation, making animated slide shows has never been easier.

To get started, check out:

  • myoglobin [1MBO]
  • dihdryofolate reductase [1RX7]
  • glucocorticoid receptor DBD [3FYL]
  • RNase [2RN2]
  • other? Just edit the url, add "/pdb/' + PDB code. (For a new structure, the server fetches the PDB from the RCSB and pre-calculates various parameters. The site is in beta so it can only handle smallish proteins/complexes and may require several reloads to work. The parameters are then cached on the server.)

Lastly, Jolecule makes it super easy to convey structural information. No more emailing PDB files with vague descriptions, just send a URL of your slide show or annotated view, the way Web 2.0 was meant to be.
